About Me

Hi, I’m Boluwatife Oyebajo — an undergraduate with a passion for technology, design, and innovation. I run Rayolink, a web design agency, where I help brands bring their ideas to life with clean, modern, and user-friendly websites. Beyond design, I’m also building PlayWall, a Web3 mobile gaming and gambling platform, blending creativity with cutting-edge technology.

Hobbies

In my free time, I like playing video games, exploring new tech ideas, and working on small design projects. I also enjoy listening to music (mostly Afrobeats and lo-fi), reading a bit of sci-fi, and going on walks when I need to clear my head.

Aspirations in Life

I aspire to build a career that blends technology, creativity, and entrepreneurship. My long-term goal is to grow my web design agency into a recognized brand that helps businesses establish strong digital identities. Beyond that, I want to create innovative projects like PlayWall, using Web3 and blockchain to shape how people interact with gaming and digital platforms. On a personal level, I hope to keep learning, improving my skills, and building opportunities not only for myself but also for others around me. Ultimately, I want to create something lasting — whether it’s a company, a product, or an idea — that makes a real impact.
